About Us

Our Mission

To raise the bar of professional childcare.
To render childcare services with high level professional standards.
To provide safe and fun learning environment for children.
To give seamless childcare support to parent's & guardians.

Kiddies Oasis is a certified childcare facility, owned by an internationally trained and certified professional childcare giver with a wealth of experience in childcare services. Our staffs are well trained in childcare giving, childcare safety and childcare first aid. We offer crèche and daycare services to aid parent's/guardians by giving best care to their children/wards within approved international and national standards of childcare giving.
At Kiddies Oasis, our facilities are tailored towards giving our children fun and entertainment while learning. We have deployed child safety measures to ensure the well being and safety of our children even as they explore their learning environment.

Our Services

Weekdays 6:30am - 6:00pm + additional 30minutes pick-up time
Open all year round except on national holidays.
Weekends 9:00am - 5:30pm
Available only on Saturdays

We offer:

   Play-based learning & school-readiness skills
   Detailed daily report (which is a snapshot of the child’s day)
   Developmental milestone checklists (tracking our children’s development)
   Reminders on children’s upcoming immunizations
   Stringent HSE & service quality standards.
   Periodic medical check-ups of children by a pediatrician.
   Brief seminars with parent's/guardians to answer questions on any areas of concern.
   Baths ,naps, laundry of children's used wears, fruits and meals ( meal is optional ).
   CCTV Surveillance.
   It’s professionally delivered childcare and childhood development service!
